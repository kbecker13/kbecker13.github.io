A Dress of Stars

#HTML #CSS #Javascript #parallax.js #Illustration

Timeline: Fall 2015, updated early 2024

Overview

A Dress of Stars is an online storybook based on the German fairy tale Die Sterntaler, which tells the tale of a young girl with next to nothing who nonetheless shares what she has with those around her. In the Brothers Grimm version of the tale (and this one, spoiler alert!), she is rewarded for her kindness and generosity and has a happy ending.

This project was initially developed as an experiment when I first discovered the parallax.js library. I was heavily inspired by the beautiful papercraft of Brittany Lee and Helen Musselwhite, and wanted to merge that with the parallax to make an effect reminiscent of a shadowbox.

While the effect works with a mouse, the immersive nature is best experienced on a tablet, where the parallax is driven by tilting the screen.

Roles

In addition to writing the website code, including the Javascript to execute parallax.js, I also created all the illustrations for the project.
